How to use:

  1. Drop, paste, or select your image.
  2. Click the color you want to remove in the left panel.
  3. Adjust Similarity and Softness to perfect the cutout.
  4. Click Save Transparent PNG.

Options & Tips:

  • 🎨 Color Mode removes the clicked color globally from the entire image.
  • 🪄 Wand Mode erases only the contiguous blob of color you clicked.
  • Edge-only mode prevents the removal of matching colors inside the subject (e.g. keeps a white shirt while removing a white background).
  • Increase Softness if you see colored halos or fringing around fine details like hair or fur.
